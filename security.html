Robust Security for Your Inventory Data

Protecting your assets with multi-layered security protocols and continuous monitoring.

Our Security Commitment

We implement comprehensive security measures at every level.

Data Encryption

Your data is encrypted both in transit (using TLS 1.2+) and at rest (using AES-256), ensuring confidentiality and integrity.

Access Control

Granular role-based access controls (RBAC) ensure users only access the data and features relevant to their roles.

Infrastructure Security

Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP) with robust physical and network security measures.

Regular Audits

Frequent internal and third-party security audits identify and address potential vulnerabilities proactively.

Threat Detection

Continuous monitoring and intrusion detection systems (IDS/IPS) help identify and mitigate threats in real-time.

Audit Logs

Comprehensive audit logs track user activity and system changes, providing accountability and traceability.

Protecting Your Business

StockWise security features are designed to safeguard your valuable inventory data, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure business continuity.

  • Secure authentication methods (including optional MFA).
  • Regular data backups and disaster recovery plans.
  • Proactive vulnerability management and patching.
  • Compliance with leading security standards.
