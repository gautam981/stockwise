Our Commitment to GDPR Compliance

Understanding and respecting your data privacy rights under the General Data Protection Regulation.

Your Data Privacy Matters

StockWise is dedicated to protecting your personal data in accordance with GDPR.

Lawfulness, Fairness, Transparency

We process your data lawfully, fairly, and transparently. You will always be informed about how your data is used.

Purpose Limitation

Data is collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.

Data Minimisation

We only collect personal data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.

Accuracy

We take reasonable steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date.

Storage Limitation

Personal data is kept in a form which permits identification of data subjects for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the data are processed.

Integrity and Confidentiality

We process personal data in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorised or unlawful processing.

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user whose data may be processed by StockWise, you have specific rights under GDPR. We are committed to upholding these rights.

Right to be Informed

About data collection and use.

Right of Access

To your personal data.

Right to Rectification

Of inaccurate data.

Right to Erasure

('Right to be forgotten').

Right to Restrict Processing

In certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability

To obtain and reuse your data.

Right to Object

To processing in certain cases.

Automated Decision Making

Rights related to profiling.
